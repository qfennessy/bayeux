6 tapestries across 6 families and 5 styles.

A demonstration of the diffusion models at nunchaku.dev. Built by Quentin Fennessy at Sundai.club on 19 April 2026. Source and architecture: github.com/qfennessy/bayeux.

Each tapestry illustrates a fictional family's history in twelve panels. Four models cooperate to produce each one:

  • Claude Opus 4.7 wrote the twelve story paragraphs for each family.
  • Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite turned each paragraph into a concise visual prompt.
  • nunchaku-qwen-image rendered each panel from its prompt.
  • Python + Pillow assembled the twelve panels into a single tapestry.

Expand the "12 panels" disclosure under each tapestry to read the story behind every image.

All family chronicles are fictional — invented to demonstrate the pipeline. Any resemblance to real people or events is coincidental.

The Chen-Vasquez Chronicle — Rembrandt style

12 panels

  1. 1865 The Chen Family Arrival

    1865 · immigration · people: Fu-hai Chen, Mei-ling Chen, Wei Chen (infant), Jian Chen, Lin-hua Chen, Hong-wei Chen, longshoremen

    In the spring of 1865, the Chen family stepped off a Pacific Mail steamer at the San Francisco wharf after a six-week voyage from Hong Kong. Father Fu-hai carried a bamboo trunk balanced on a shoulder pole, and mother Mei-ling held baby Wei against the cold Pacific wind. The harbor was crowded with gulls, longshoremen, and piled crates of tea and silk.

    Image prompt

  2. 1867 Jian Chen in the Sierra Nevada

    1867 · labor · people: Jian Chen, fellow Chinese railroad laborers

    Eldest son Jian was hired onto the Central Pacific Railroad, chipping granite inside a half-finished Sierra Nevada tunnel. He swung a heavy hammer by lantern light beside other Chinese laborers, their queues tied up under knit caps. Beyond the tunnel mouth, snowdrifts leaned against the wooden scaffolds.

    Image prompt

Flickering lanterns cast long, dramatic shadows. Beyond the tunnel opening, massive snowdrifts press against timber scaffolds, creating a harsh, industrious, and frozen atmosphere.
